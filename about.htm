About the Organization

Vandy_LAN: The Vanderbilt Gaming Club has been founded and is dedicated to promote and encourage:

  • diversity of the community
  • opportunities to engage in university-wide efforts to bolster community
  • alcohol-free late night programming
  • interest and passion for video games and technology
